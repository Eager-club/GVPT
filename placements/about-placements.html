Training and placement

Training and Placement (T&P) cell is an integral part of all the institutions. Gayatri Vidya Parishad has been strengthened over the years and achieved many milestones in its journey towards Quality and quantity of students who are placed from the various institutions of Gayatri Vidya Parishad.

our aim:

The aim of the T & P cell is to mould all the students of the institution to meet the industry expectations and place them into top companies in the industry. To achieve these objectives the cell organizes and coordinates Campus Recruitment Training (CRT) programs with a commitment of placing every students in a reputed company. These programmes help the students acquire required technical skills, develop personality and focus on holistic development. They are designed to give the society, better citizens as per our mission statement.

Our strengths:

Our students who have excelled themselves in the academics also displayed ethical and moral values picked up during their tenure in the college, thus making themselves self learning and self evolving into good Samaritans. The success stories of their contributions to the organizations who picked them up from the campus, have new and better companies to the campus and enhanced their trust and belief in our institutions.

our training:

A thorough and complete awareness is given to the students on various career programmes and certifications as per the current demands. Accordingly, appropriate resource persons are identified and training sessions are planned in such a way that their curriculum is not disturbed.
